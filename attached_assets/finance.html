Portfolio Analyzer

A web-based portfolio management and analysis tool designed for Indian stock market and mutual fund investors. Users can upload their stock, crypto, and mutual fund portfolios via CSV or manual entries and get real-time performance analysis, profit/loss tracking and Risk Analysis. The app also features SIP/SWP calculators, financial news, and personalized asset allocation recommendations based on risk and return. Built with Python (Flask) backend and HTML/CSS frontend, deployed on Render.
